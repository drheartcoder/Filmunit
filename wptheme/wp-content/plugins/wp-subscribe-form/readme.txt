Subscribe Form allows you to create and manage powerful promotion Subscribe Forms for WordPress blog or website with Mailchimp ActiveCampaign & GetResponse


== Description ==

Subscribe Form allows you to create and manage powerful promotion Subscribe Forms for WordPress blog or website. Powerful, and yet, easy to use Subscribe Form plugin that will help you to grab your visitor's attention to introduce him offers, discounts or other kind of promotional notices.

Create amazing and beautiful Subscribe Forms using Subscribe Form plugin with MailChimp, ActiveCampaign and GetResponse Support, 12 pre designed and ready to use Subscribe Forms for your WordPress site to increase your subscribers. You can customize everything if you want your color scheme and background images in Subscribe Form. Pre-defined options help you to change the look and feel of Subscribe Forms and make it yours.

Subscribe Form Plugin is very easy to use. Subscribe Form is designed in such a way so you don't need to spend much time to activate or customize Subscribe Form, with provided guidelines and other descriptive text with options make it easy to setup a new Subscribe Form, with just few clicks and some minutes of your time you will get your Subscribe Form up and running on your WordPress site.

> We created the [premium version](http://www.arrowplugins.com/subscribe-form) with many extra features and an optimized interface, so it's even more intuitive and quick to design beautifully customized subscribe forms and multiple types subscribe forms. It will be well worth of your investment!

<strong>Why You Should Consider Upgrading to [Subscribe Form Premium](http://www.arrowplugins.com/subscribe-form)</strong> 

If you've been using our free Subscribe Form plugin, then you already love the awesome functionality and the ability to create Subscribe Forms and you might be wondering what’s included in our premium version to justify the upgrade?

On top of the incredibly flexible new design templates that allow you to customize your Subscribe Form to your heart's content... we've also included new ways to customize the user experience.

<strong>Highly Customizable: </strong>Every Subscribe Form comes with many customizations, you can change anything of selected Subscribe Form like heading text, color, size, overlay transparency, background color, background image, border color, action button text, action button color, action button text color and many more....

<strong>MailChimp Support: </strong>To convert your visitors into subscribers, Subscribe Form comes with MailChimp Support, you just need your API Key & List ID

<strong>ActiveCampaign Support: </strong>To convert your visitors into subscribers.

<strong>GetResponse Support: </strong>To convert your visitors into subscribers.

<strong>Subscribe Form Templates: </strong>Subscribe Form comes with 12 pre designed templates which are customizable including for Newsletters, Discount, General Subscribe Forms, Shopping Templates and more....


* 12 Pre Designed Subscribe Forms.
* Highly Customizable!
* Mobile Friendly and fully Responsive for Mobile Devices.
* Ajax support to submit form.
* Save Subscribers to MailChimp, ActiveCampaign and GetResponse directly.
* Save subscribers to local database.
* Mail the subscriber's info to any email adress.
* General Subscribe Form Templates.
* Newsletter Subscribe Form Templates.
* Shopping Subscribe Form Templates.
* Discount Subscribe Form Templates and many more....
* Change Subscribe Form text size and color.
* Change the background color of Subscribe Form.
* Change the background image of Subscribe Form.
* Change the width of Subscribe Form.
* Show Subscribe Form with Shortcode anywhere.
* Show success message option after subscribed.
* Invalid e-mail error message.
* Auto form error validation included.
* Missing field error message option.
* Delete the subscriber's list from local database.
* Delete single subscriber entry from local database.
* Export Subscribers to CSV file to import in MailChimp or other mailing services.

Customizing the look and feel of the Subscribe Form is as important as the content itself. That's why Subscribe Form gives you the ability to customize the timings and size of the Subscribe Form to your needs.

Subscribe Form team constantly works on upgrades and improvements. With our upcoming updates we are planning to add more types of Subscribe Forms.

Thank you for using our Subscribe Form plugin.

If you think that you found a bug in our Subscribe Form plugin or have any questions, please feel free to contact us at arrowplugins@gmail.com

== Installation ==

### First download the ZIP file ,

1. Log in to your website administrator panel.   
2. Go to Plugins page, and add new plugin.    
3. Upload Subscribe Form plugin 
4. Click `Install Now` button.     
5. Then click `Activate Plugin` button.
